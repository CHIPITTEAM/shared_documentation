Good to know - Rally Bar Remote
The small remote on the table is the Logitech Rally Bar Remote. Use it to adjust the room camera and speaker volume - no need to touch the TAP IP on the wall.
The camera automatically follows whoever is speaking. If the angle looks off, use the remote to adjust it.
Quick troubleshooting
No "ClickShare" option in Teams? Make sure the ClickShare app is running on your laptop, check the system tray.
TV screen is black? Check that the TV is set to HDMI 1.
Camera blurry or off-centre? Use the Rally Bar Remote on the table to adjust the camera view.
Button won't turn red? Unplug the button, wait 5 seconds, and plug it back in.
