Teams Rum 📹

Hurtig guide  ·  CHIP-IT
Sadan starter du et mode
1
Book lokalet i din kalender
Tilfoj lokalets navn som deltager i din Teams- eller Outlook-invitation. Lokalet accepterer automatisk, hvis det er ledigt.
2
Ga ind i lokalet
Dit mode vises automatisk pa betjeningspanelet pa bordet.
3
Tryk "Join" pa betjeningspanelet
Kamera, mikrofon og skaerm er klar med det samme. Ingen opsaetning nodvendig.
Vil du dele din skaerm?
A
Tag din laptop med og deltag i modet
Abn modet i Teams pa din laptop og klik "Join".
⚠️  Sluk lyden pa din laptop, inden du deltager
Vaelg "Don't use audio" nar Teams sporger om lydindstilling - ellers far du ekko. Rummet klarer al lyd.
B
Del din skaerm fra Teams
Klik pa Share-ikonet i Teams pa din laptop og vaelg, hvad du vil vise.

Teams Room 📹

Quick guide  ·  CHIP-IT
How to start a meeting
1
Book the room in your calendar
Add the room name as an attendee in your Teams or Outlook invite. The room accepts automatically if it is free.
2
Walk into the room
Your meeting appears automatically on the touch panel on the table.
3
Tap "Join" on the touch panel
Camera, microphone and screen are ready instantly. No setup needed.
Want to share your screen?
A
Bring your laptop and join the meeting
Open the meeting in Teams on your laptop and click "Join".
⚠️  Mute your laptop audio before joining
Choose "Don't use audio" when Teams asks about audio settings - otherwise you will get echo. The room handles all sound.
B
Share your screen from Teams
Click the Share icon in Teams on your laptop and choose what to present.
