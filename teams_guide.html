1. Book the Room

1. Book lokalet

Do this in Teams or Outlook before the meeting

Gor dette i Teams eller Outlook for modet

1
Create a new meeting in Teams or Outlook Opret et nyt mode i Teams eller Outlook

Use the calendar and click New meeting.

Brug kalenderen og klik pa Nyt mode.

2
Add the room as a Required Attendee Tilfoj lokalet som paakraevet deltager

Search for the room name (e.g. Lunchroom 5.08) in the attendee field - just like inviting a colleague.

Sog efter lokalets navn (f.eks. Frokostrum 5.08) i deltagerfeltet - ligesom du inviterer en kollega.

The room accepts automatically if it is free.

Lokalet accepterer automatisk, hvis det er ledigt.

💡 Shortcut: clicking the Location field shows a list of all available CHIP rooms to pick from.

💡 Genvej: klik pa Location-feltet for at se en liste over alle ledige CHIP-lokaler at vaelge imellem.

3
Send the invite Send invitationen

Your meeting is now linked to the room screen - it will appear on the touch panel when you arrive.

Dit mode er nu knyttet til lokalets skaerm - det vises pa betjeningspanelet, nar du ankommer.

💡
You can also check room availability directly in the Outlook Room Finder panel on the right side when creating an event.
Du kan ogsa tjekke lokalets ledighed direkte i Outlook Lokalesogning-panelet til hojre, nar du opretter en begivenhed.
▶️

2. Join the Meeting

2. Deltag i modet

Walk in - the room is ready for you

Ga ind - lokalet er klar til dig

1
Find your meeting on the touch screen Find dit mode pa beroeringsskaermen

The table's touch panel shows all upcoming meetings for this room.

Bordets betjeningspanel viser alle kommende moder for dette lokale.

2
Tap "Join" Tryk pa "Join"

One tap and the camera, microphone and screen are all active. No setup needed.

Et tryk og kameraet, mikrofonen og skaermen er alle aktive. Ingen opsaetning krevet.

📷 Touch panel - tap "Join" next to your meeting:

📷 Betjeningspanel - tryk "Join" ved siden af dit mode:

💡
The room is the host. You do not need to connect your laptop to camera, speakers, or microphone. Just bring yourself.
Lokalet er vaerten. Du behover ikke tilslutte din laptop til kamera, hojttalere eller mikrofon. Tag bare dig selv med.
⚠️
Using Jens' Office? That room cannot be booked - just walk in and tap Meet now on the touch panel to start an instant meeting.
Bruger du Jens' kontor? Det lokale kan ikke bookes - ga bare ind og tryk pa Meet now pa betjeningspanelet for at starte et ojeblikkeligt mode.
📺

3. Share Your Screen

3. Del din skaerm

Join the meeting from your laptop, then share from there

Deltag i modet fra din laptop, og del derfra

1
Bring your laptop and join the meeting - without audio Tag din laptop med og deltag i modet - uden lyd

Open the meeting in Teams on your laptop and click Join. Before entering, Teams will ask about audio - select Don't use audio to avoid echo. The room handles all sound.

Abn modet i Teams pa din laptop og klik pa Join. For du deltager, sporger Teams om lyd - vaelg Don't use audio for at undga ekko. Rummet klarer al lyd.

2
Share your screen from your laptop Del din skaerm fra din laptop

Click the Share icon in Teams and choose what to present. It appears on the room display and for all remote participants.

Klik pa Share-ikonet i Teams og vaelg, hvad du vil vise. Det vises pa lokalets skaerm og for alle fjerndeltagere.

💡
You do not need to use your laptop's camera or microphone - the room handles both. Your laptop is only needed if you want to share content.
Du behover ikke bruge din laptops kamera eller mikrofon - rummet klarer begge dele. Din laptop er kun nodvendig, hvis du vil dele indhold.
🌐

4. Inviting External Participants

4. Inviter eksterne deltagere

The invite must be sent from the organiser's laptop

Invitationen skal sendes fra arrangørens laptop

1
Add their email address to the invite (on your laptop) Tilfoj deres emailadresse til invitationen (pa din laptop)

In Teams or Outlook on your laptop, add the external email (e.g. name@gmail.com) as an attendee before the meeting. Teams will label them as External.

I Teams eller Outlook pa din laptop skal du tilføje den eksterne email (f.eks. name@gmail.com) som deltager inden mødet. Teams markerer dem som Ekstern.

2
They receive a join link by email De modtager et link via email

The invite email contains a Join Microsoft Teams Meeting link. They click it - no Teams account or app required.

Invitations-emailen indeholder et Deltag i Microsoft Teams-mode-link. De klikker pa det - ingen Teams-konto eller app krevet.

3
They wait in the lobby De venter i lobbyen

External guests land in a virtual waiting room. You will see a notification on the touch panel to admit them.

Eksterne gaester havner i et virtuelt ventelokale. Du vil se en besked pa betjeningspanelet om at lukke dem ind.

4
Admit them from the lobby Indslaep dem fra lobbyen

When you see their name, tap Admit on the touch panel or click it in Teams on your laptop. They join the call immediately.

Nar du ser deres navn, tryk pa Admit pa betjeningspanelet eller klik pa det i Teams pa din laptop. De deltager i opkaldet med det samme.

External clicks link Ekstern klikker pa link

⚠️
Nobody admitted them? If the organizer does not admit the guest within a few minutes, Teams will send a reminder notification. Check your Teams notifications if you do not see the lobby popup.
Ingen har indslaept dem? Hvis arrangoren ikke indslaeper gaesten inden for et par minutter, sender Teams en pamindelsesbesked. Tjek dine Teams-notifikationer, hvis du ikke ser lobby-popupen.

Joining as an External Guest

Deltag som ekstern gaest

Share this section with people outside the organisation

Del dette afsnit med personer uden for organisationen

1
Open the invitation email Aben invitations-emailen

Find the email from the meeting organiser and click Join Microsoft Teams Meeting.

Find emailen fra modearrangoren og klik pa Deltag i Microsoft Teams-mode.

2
Choose how to join Vaelg, hvordan du vil deltage

Your browser opens a page with two options:

Din browser abner en side med to muligheder:

Open in Teams app - if you have Teams installed (recommended for best quality)
Continue in browser - no install needed, works in Chrome or Edge

Abn i Teams-appen - hvis du har Teams installeret (anbefalet for bedste kvalitet)
Fortsaet i browser - ingen installation krevet, virker i Chrome eller Edge

3
Enter your name and join Indtast dit navn og deltag

Type your name so participants can identify you, then click Join now.

Skriv dit navn, sa deltagerne kan genkende dig, og klik pa Deltag nu.

4
Wait to be admitted Vent pa at blive indslaept

You will see a "Waiting to be admitted" screen. The host will let you in shortly.

Du vil se en "Venter pa at blive indslaept"-skaerm. Vaerten slipper dig ind om lidt.

✓
You do not need a Microsoft account or a Teams subscription. The browser version is free and requires no installation.
Du behover ikke en Microsoft-konto eller et Teams-abonnement. Browserversionen er gratis og kraever ingen installation.
⚠️
Camera or microphone not working? When the browser asks for permission to use your camera and microphone, click Allow. If you missed it, click the padlock icon in the browser address bar to reset permissions.
Kamera eller mikrofon virker ikke? Nar browseren beder om tilladelse til at bruge kamera og mikrofon, skal du klikke pa Tillad. Hvis du forpassede det, skal du klikke pa hanelasikonet i browserens adresselinje for at nulstille tilladelserne.
